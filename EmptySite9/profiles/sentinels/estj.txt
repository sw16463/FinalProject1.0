ESTJs are representatives of tradition and order, utilizing their understanding 
of what is right, wrong and socially acceptable to bring families and communities
together. Embracing the values of honesty, dedication and dignity, people with
the ESTJ personality type are valued for their clear advice and guidance, and 
they happily lead the way on difficult paths. Taking pride in bringing people 
together, ESTJs often take on roles as community organizers, working hard to
bring everyone together in celebration of cherished local events, or
in defense of the traditional values that hold families and communities together.
